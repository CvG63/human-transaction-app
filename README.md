The Human Transaction
The Human Transaction is an interactive digital environment designed to help navigate the crossroads between the life you inherited and the life you choose.
Drawing on the mechanics of somatic regulation, meaning-making, and deep self-compassion, it provides a structured path for examining old adaptations and building a life from conscious choice. The framework centers on radical grace—the profound self-forgiveness required when releasing outdated survival strategies—and the deliberate architecture of becoming someone new.
The Framework
The experience is divided into 18 chapters following five stages of the Hero's Journey:
The Inherited Map: Examining the beliefs and constraints given to us before we could choose.
The Armor and the Echo: Understanding our intelligent adaptations with curiosity rather than judgment.
The Descent and the Grace: The mechanics of rebirth and extending radical self-forgiveness.
The Conscious Architecture: Building a life from chosen values, clear systems, and a regulated presence.
The Open Horizon: Integrating the shadow, finding meaning, and engaging in ongoing mastery.
Technical Architecture
Built to feel like a sanctuary on the web, the application prioritizes visual stillness and focus. It is constructed entirely in Vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
Zero Dependencies: No frameworks, libraries, or build steps required.
Single-File App: The entire experience lives in one perfectly contained file.
Thoughtful UX: Features custom in-memory routing, dynamic progress tracking, keyboard accessibility, and local storage to quietly remember which chapters have been read.
